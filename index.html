About Kehler Vegetable Company

Located in the heart of Black Creek, our small family farm is growing great things for our local community. You will find the freshest produce, berries and non-gmo eggs from our pasture in our farmstand seasonally each year. The farmstand runs on the honour system and is stocked up daily right at the farm gate!

What We Offer

  • Root Vegetables: Carrots, potatoes, onions.
  • Seasonal Vegetables: Squash, pumpkins.
  • non-gmo eggs
  • Additional Items: Fruits and herbs (availability depends on the season).
